Zachary Blouin

Becoming a game developer has always been a dream of mine. I have pursued video games for most of my life, but I always wondered what technical skills were needed to build them. In 2018, I took my first professional steps towards this dream when I started working on Commanders of Valor for Moonlit Sky Games. This hands-on game development experience helped my passions grow. Since then, my passions have only grown bigger as I developed new games and experience. Throughout my projects in this showcase, each of them has offered me their own challenges to overcome. None the less, I am always committed to seeing projects though to the finish line. My passion for game development resonates best with team members that are as highly motivated as I am. I highly value strong leadership in producers and mangers, however, I am not afraid to take the leadership role to support the team.


This website is a perfect showcase of the many projects I have worked or managed, my skills and my resume. I am eager to keep to learning and create so much more.
