Zachary Blouin

Becoming a game developer has always been my dream.

In 2018, I took my first professional steps towards this dream when I started working on Commanders of Valor for Moonlit Sky Games. This hands-on game development experience helped nurture my passion for game development as I continued to grow. My drive to create burned even greater as I picked up work at Gasket Games where I developed new games, experiences and patches.

Within this portfolio I have tried my best to demonstrate some of the challenges to I have had to overcome the skills I have learned along the way. My passion for game development resonates best with team members that are as equally excited to see a project succeed as I am. I highly value strong leadership in producers and mangers, however, I am not afraid to take the reins to when I need support my team!

This website is my faithful showcase to the highlights of my game development career and is serves as a living tour of the many projects I have worked on.
